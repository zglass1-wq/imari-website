The grounds.

One private acre in the heart of Georgetown. Gardens originally designed by Rose Greely in 1930 — later updated by Perry Wheeler, designer of the White House Rose Garden — including terraces, grand estate walls, a bowling green, a large herb garden, and original boxwoods. Detached spa house and full-size pool.
