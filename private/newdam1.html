The Property

A 12,000 sq ft house on a walled, gated acre of gardens in Georgetown. Six suites, a 1,000 sq ft ballroom, a spa house, and a separate guest house for client staff.

  • 12,000 sq ft main house on one private acre in Georgetown D.C.
  • Manicured gardens and greenhouse designed by Rose Greely (1930), updated by Perry Wheeler — designer of the White House Rose Garden
  • Gated arrival court with parking for 10 vehicles
  • In the main house: 6 bedroom suites across two floors with 15 bathrooms
  • Entertainment & living spaces flow naturally across the first floor — Parlor, Living Room, Orangerie, and formal Dining Room
  • Chef's Kitchen — commercial-grade, built for serious cooking at any scale
  • Elevator in main residence
  • Private gym, dedicated yoga/meditation room, full-size swimming pool, and detached Spa House with sauna, steam room, cold plunge, and hot tub
  • Detached 1,000 sq ft ballroom
  • Multiple indoor and outdoor spaces for breakouts, receptions, and gatherings
  • Three-bedroom guest house — separate entrance and self-contained — for client staff (security, EAs, nannies). Available at additional cost.

The Service

A full team on property from arrival to departure — concierge, butler, housekeeping, Michelin-star chefs — coordinating everything so the client doesn't have to ask twice.

  • 24/7 dedicated concierge managing all logistics end-to-end — planning, reservations, transportation, and on-site coordination
  • Butler-led arrival, daily service, and evening turndown
  • Full housekeeping team — daily service and full property reset after events and overnight
  • In-residence garment care — laundry, pressing, steaming
  • Property team functions as an extension of the planner's own team
  • Farm-sourced breakfast daily — fresh fruit, eggs made to order, pastries, organic yogurt, cold-pressed juices, coffee, tea
  • Stocked kitchen customized in advance to guest preferences
  • Michelin-star chefs available for meals and private dinners
  • Welcome champagne, unpacking service, and private guided estate introduction on arrival
  • Custom floral arrangements styled throughout the estate prior to arrival

The Venue

A purpose-built venue for hosting, from 24-person dinners to 300-person receptions. Full AV, full production, full team.

  • Up to 60 seated at king's tables or 70 at rounds in the ballroom
  • Up to 24 for private dinners in the formal dining room
  • Up to 300 for larger receptions and cocktail events across the property
  • Full event planning and production — ideation through execution
  • Full AV suite for presentations, panels, and production
  • Custom menus designed for the occasion and guests
  • Flow from work session to cocktail hour to dinner without changing venues

The Security

Whole-property exclusivity, private arrival, perimeter cameras, NDA-bound staff. Vetted and used by heads of state.

  • Complete property exclusivity — no other guests, no lobby, no shared spaces

  • Integrated estate-wide security system with perimeter PTZ cameras, designed and vetted for heads of state and senior officials
  • All staff bound by confidentiality agreement before entering the property
  • Armed security personnel available
